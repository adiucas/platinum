UI/UX Designer
Vacancy
01
Job Context
Platinum Group is looking for creative and passionate UI/UX designer.
Job Responsibilities
  • Develop UI/UX solutions using a user-centered design.
  • Conduct research, User flows, architecture, Visual design based on requirements.
  • Collaborate with the Project Managers, Software engineers, Stakeholders, QA Team, the Marketing team for design and decision-related meetings.
  • Develop user flows, and site maps to demonstrate design ideas.
  • Create final UI screens for several resolutions (whenever required).
  • Creative content development for Marketing and Advertising Campaigns such as social media posts, banners, blog images, info graphics, etc.
  • Generate wire frames, interface, and iterate on them as per project requirements.
  • Propose ideas by following standard design principles, pattern, typography, design trends, etc.
  • Need to communicate with clients and convert requirements into UI design and prototyping.
  • Need to communicate with development team to explain the design.
  • Keep up to date knowledge on design trends, technology, tools & resources.
Employment Status
Full-time
Educational Requirements
Graduated in CSE or diploma in Arts & Design or relevant subject.
Experience Requirements
At least 4 year(s)
Additional Requirements
  • Age at least 25 years
  • Both males and females are allowed to apply
  • Proficiency in Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Figma, Adobe XD, Principle, Sketch.
  • Strong experience in Website, Web Application Design. Also,need expertise knowledge of iOS and Android mobile application design.
  • Knowledge of building and maintaining a Design System and deliver design assets to developers.
  • Eager to learn independently with no/minimum supervision.
  • Pro-active, self-motivated and eager to work in challenging environment.
  • Eager to learn independently with no/minimum supervision.
  • Excellent communication skill in English.
