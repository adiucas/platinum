Company Overview

Platinum City was founded on 2011. It is located just beside the Japan Economic Zone. The location of the project is 160 feet adjacent to the Dhaka-Sylhet highway. Platinum City is an area which has the combination of both urbanized and natural beauty. Platinum City there is advanced urban planning concepts at satellite cities in which all of the civic demand drivers are available in every plot. We have 100% uninterrupted land that has ISO certification 9001: 2006.


Our project is located in a completely flood-free area where there is no possibility of water logging. We have 3, 4, 5 and 10 Katha plot area. The project is located 180 feet adjacent to the Dhaka-Sylhet highway. Our Platinum City registered under National Housing Authority (Registration No.0013). We are the member of Real Estate and Housing Association of Bangladesh (Rehab) (a member of the No -078).


It's a luxury residential project by approved skilled architects, environmentalists and urban planners in accordance with government policy. As the Platinum City project is close to Purbachal New Town, a modern suburb designed by RAJUK, all government facilities will be available to the plot holders.
