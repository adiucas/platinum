company profile

about us

Platinum Business Consortium, a sister concern of Platinum Group is a foreign trade and local company which is understood by its name and deals with import and export in general including infrastructures, Power Section, Fire Safety and Protection, building up Steel Structures, Information Technology and All Kind Supply Items including Heavy equipment’s and Industrial Machines in Public and Government Sector including Private Corporate Sectors. Our company has primarily focused on import and export of electrical & lighting products on which it has many years’ experience. Later on, as demand from the market and new opportunities emerged, we started importing and exporting some others consumer products, spare parts of mobile phone and other electronics accessories along with light and heavy equipment’s.


Platinum Business Consortium, a sister concern of Platinum Group has its own registered brand "Platinum" and is getting its various Products. We can keep the pulse of the market as a result of our strong business cooperation with reputable Turkish and oversea companies and we are able to supply the best quality products and services at affordable prices. In the near future, our company also aims to establish regional dealerships in Bangladesh and focus on import and export in general including infrastructures, Power Section, Fire Safety and Protection, building up Steel Structures, Information Technology and All Kind Supply Items including Heavy equipment’s and Industrial Machines in Public and Government Sector including Private Corporate Sectors related activities with its own branded (Platinum) products as well as the products of the producers that already have business cooperation with us.

company & product overview

Platinum Business Consortium, a sister concern of Platinum Group is a general trading firm specializing in sourcing and marketing of various raw materials and finshed products. The company has various multinational clients in the industry sector such as food, agro-industry, petro chemicals, equipments, spare parts of electronics item and packaging materials which they distribute their raw materials and finished food as well as doing business in import and export in general including infrastructures, Power Section, Fire Safety and Protection, building up Steel Structures, Information Technology and All Kind Supply Items including Heavy equipment’s and Industrial Machines in Public and Government Sector including Private Corporate Sectors. The company imports directly and sells to their clients. They also distribute their products in various areas all over the country.
